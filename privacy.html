Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Victoria AI collects the following types of information to provide our services:

  • Location Data: GPS coordinates, city, state, and general location information for location-aware threat monitoring.
  • Contact Information: Phone number and email address for sending alerts and communications.
  • Communication Data: Messages, voice calls, and interactions with the Victoria AI system.
  • Device Information: Battery level, device type, and GPS accuracy for system optimization.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with Victoria AI, preferences, and settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing real-time threat monitoring and safety alerts
  • Sending SMS notifications for critical events
  • Personalizing the AI assistant experience
  • Improving system performance and accuracy
  • Location-aware threat detection and filtering
  • Maintaining conversation history and memory

3. Data Storage and Security

Victoria AI takes data security seriously:

  • All data is stored locally on secure systems
  • Location data is encrypted during transmission
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized users only
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties
  • Regular security audits and updates are performed

4. SMS Communications

Regarding SMS alert services:

  • You must explicitly opt-in to receive SMS alerts
  • You can opt-out at any time by replying STOP to any message
  • Message frequency varies based on threat levels and your preferences
  • Standard message and data rates may apply
  • We only send alerts for significant threats and important updates

5. Third-Party Services

Victoria AI uses the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude API: For AI conversation and analysis
  • Twilio: For SMS and voice communications
  • Brave Search API: For real-time news and information
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim: For location geocoding

These services have their own privacy policies and terms of service.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Location history: 100 most recent updates
  • Conversation history: Indefinitely for personalization
  • Threat alerts: 30 days
  • SMS logs: 7 days

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting victoria.e.arnold@protonmail.com

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of SMS communications at any time
  • Disable location tracking
  • Export your data in a readable format

8. Children's Privacy

Victoria AI is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
